Our Story

ArtVision is a contemporary art collective based in Nairobi, Kenya, dedicated to promoting African artists and fostering creative connections across the continent and beyond.

SERVICES

What We Offer

We provide a range of services to support artists and connect them with collectors, institutions, and art enthusiasts.

Artist Representation

Professional representation and career development for emerging and established artists.

Curatorial Services

Exhibition design, curation, and production for galleries and institutions.

Art Advisory

Personalized art advisory services for collectors and corporate clients.
